Gavel

Made a gavel out of two precut turning blocks for my stepmom who works at the surpreme court as a Christmas gift for her(2021). I used a wood lathe and made the gavels head piece, and then did the same for the handle on the lathe with nice slopes for holding the gavel. I then used a drill press to make a hole in the head for the handle to attach, which I did with wood glue. To finish it off I used some mahoghany wood stain to give it a nice rich color that made the dark brown/red stand out and it's a gift that I am extremely proud of.
