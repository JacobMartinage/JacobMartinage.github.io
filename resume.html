About Me

Goals

Ever since I was a kid, my favorite show has been the original MacGyver series. MacGyver perfectly captures my love for innovation and ingenuity, and despite being fictional, is someone I have looked up to since I was a kid. I loved the way he found ingenious solutions to get out of precarious situations and fell in love with his style of problem-solving. I try to spend as much of my free time as possible on projects to develop skills in craftsmanship and CS, utilizing my creative side in the process. I love taking random parts and pieces and forming them together to create a beautiful or unusual final product. These projects, albeit somewhat goofy, are my way of expressing creativity and exploring my passions. I hope to become a MacGyver of the modern era by getting a Masters in electrical and computer engineering(ECE), becoming well-versed in both hardware and software, giving me the ability to find creative solutions to any problem I come across, as well as the ability to create any goofy project I want.
