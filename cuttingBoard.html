Cutting Boards

In December 2022 I was at my Grandfather's house and wanted to make some gifts for Christmas and after a little bit of searching decided that cutting boards would make perfect gifts! I cut up a ton of different piece of mixed wood and lined up 3 different cutting boards. I then used a planer to get them all to the same thickness and then glued them together, and once they dried I created a flat edge using a table saw, and ran them through the planer 1-2 more times to get off all the glue and ensure they were even. I then sanded them for forever and a day, and for the final touch covered them in cutting board oil, which made the colors in the wood pop, and also made them food safe.
